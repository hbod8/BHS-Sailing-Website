Equipment

Here is a list of the gear you will need to be comfortable and safe as we sail this season.

Mandatory:

  • Type 3 personal flotation device (PFD) in good condition, that fits well.
  • Waterproof jacket
  • Waterproof pants
  • Fleece or wool hat
  • Sunscreen

This gear needs to come to all regattas and practices.

Other stuff you may want to have:

  • Sailing booties (you can use old sneakers and wool socks instead)
  • Polypro inner layers
  • Sailing gloves
  • Sunglasses
  • A waterproof watch is very helpful

    • Gear may be obtained from many sources:

    • West Coast Sailing (This web-based retailer offers a 15% discount for registered high school team members, free shipping on most items, and no sales tax)
    • Annapolis Performance Sailing (This web-based retailer offers a 15% discount for registered high school team members, and no sales tax)
    • Layline.com
    • Fisheries Supply in Seattle at Lake Union (Refer to the Bainbridge High School Sailing Team corporate account for a discount of approximately 10%)
    • West Marine in Seattle or Bremerton
    • REI Seattle

    There are a lot of sailors who outgrow stuff in the four years with the team and you may be able to hunt up some used gear. E-mail appeals to other BHS Sailing families have worked well in the past.

    Team Boats

    The team uses Vanguard 15 and Flying Junior (FJ) sailboats for practices and regattas. These boats are designed for "two-handed" (i.e., 2 crew members) racing.

    The Bock

    A floating / powered Boat Dock, called a "Bock," is used for local regattas and practices.
